Cheese Cake

For the dough:

  • 200g flour
  • 75g sugar
  • 75g margarine
  • 1 egg
  • 7,5 g baking powder
  • fat for the mold

For the filling:

  • 125g margarine
  • 225g sugar
  • 1 sachet of vanilla sugar
  • 1 sachet of vanilla custard powder
  • 3 eggs
  • 500g quark
  • 200g sour creme
  • 200g sweet cream

Place the ingredients for the dough in a bowl, knead together quickly and set aside.

For the filling, mix the margarine, sugar, vanilla sugar, custard powder and eggs in a bowl. Then mix in the quark and mix in the quark and sour cream. Whip the sweet cream until stiff and fold in.

Preheat the oven to 180 Â°C top/bottom heat.

Spread the dough in a greased 26 cm cake tin, raising it 2 - 3 cm up the sides. Now pour the filling into the tin, smooth it out and bake in a hot oven on the second shelf from the bottom for 1 hour.

Caution: Do not remove the cake from the tin until it has cooled completely, as the consistency of the quark mixture is still too soft immediately after removing it from the oven.
